# ✅ Social Media Do's

1. Enable Privacy Settings: Regularly review your account settings to control who can see your personal posts and information.

2. Think Before You Post: Remember that digital footprints are often permanent; ensure your content reflects the professional image you want to project.

3. Use Professional Visuals: Use clear, professional-looking profile pictures, especially on career-oriented platforms like LinkedIn.

4. Fact-Check Before Sharing: Verify the authenticity of news or links before reposting to prevent the spread of misinformation.

5. Network Purposefully: Use social media to follow industry leaders, join academic groups, and connect with alumni in your field of study.

# ❌ Social Media Don'ts

1. Don’t Share Sensitive Info: Never post your home address, phone number, or current real-time location (checking in) for safety reasons.

2. Don’t Engage in Cyberbullying: Avoid participating in online arguments, harassment, or sharing content that targets or demeans others.

3. Don’t Post While Angry: Avoid "venting" about professors, classmates, or internship coordinators, as this can be seen by future employers.

4. Don’t Tag Without Permission: Never tag friends or colleagues in photos or videos that might be considered embarrassing or unprofessional.

5. Don’t Click Suspicious Links: Avoid clicking on "clickbait" or unknown links in your feed, as these are common methods for phishing and identity theft.
